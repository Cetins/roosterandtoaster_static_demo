About Us


Small plates with big flavours

Welcome to Rooster & Toaster, where we craft culinary experiences that blend the rich traditions of Turkish and Lebanese cuisine. Our passion for exquisite flavors and cultural fusion drives us to create memorable dining moments for our clients. At Rooster & Toaster, we specialize in mezze, offering a tantalizing array of small plates bursting with bold and authentic tastes. With each dish meticulously prepared, we bring the essence of both Turkish and Lebanese culinary heritage to every event.

Whether you're hosting an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, allow us to elevate your experience with our unparalleled expertise and dedication to exceptional service. Discover the magic of our culinary journey and let us delight your senses with our small plates and big flavors.
